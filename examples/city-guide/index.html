Restaurants

Mai Lee

Vietnamese restaurant in Brentwood

Mai Lee is a delicious Vietnamese restaurant in Brentwood. It has a very exapnsive menu including Pho, Bahn Mi sandwiches, noodle dishes, as well as traditional Chinese fare. Make sure to try the Vietnamese coffee!

Byrd & Barrel

Fried chicken restaurant in South City

Byrd & Barrel is a fried chicken place on Jefferson Ave. in south city St. Louis. They have several chicken menu items such as "nugz", wings, and sandwiches. They also feature delicious, inventive side dieshes.

Carl's Deli

Classic deli in Clayton

Carl's Deli is a classic delicatessen on Clayton Road in St. Louis, near the WashU campus. They have a menu of deli sandwiches which are all out of this world. Make sure to try the Pastrami sandwich!

Entertainment

St. Louis Blues

Best hockey team in the entire world.

St. Louis Blues is professional hockey team in St. Louis. They play in the Scottrade Center downtown, and games are an unforgettable experience.

Plaza Frontenac Cinema

Small theater that shows independent films.

Plaza Frontenac Cinema is a small theater that shows recently released independent films. It is an intimate venue and has a great selection of snacks and beverages.

Concert venue in The Loop.
